Hello, I'm Caleb Foster!

With over 14 years of experience in track and field, 4 years running at the University of Florida, and 1 at the University of Oregon I've had the privilege of competing at elite levels from Collegiate to national and now post-collegiate. My coaching philosophy centers on a holistic and intentional approach, focused not just on physical mechanics and strength but also on mental resilience, nutrition, and personalized strategies and habits.

I believe that every athlete has unique potential waiting to be unlocked by the right coach. My small group sessions are designed to create a supportive, competitive environment where you can push your limits and achieve your personal best while my 1-on-1 sessions are even more technical and personal. Let's work together to get you to the next level and forming the habits that are likely to lead you to success.

"All it takes is one."

This can mean one practice, one talk, one que, one jump, or one step. All the "1s" add up to form the result you end up with.
